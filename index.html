How It Works

1. Capture

Put on the DexterSense glove and glasses, run quick auto-calibration (≈2 min), and record human demos with on-screen event markers.

2. Generate Dataset

Our system fuses the glove’s joint angles with video from the glasses to produce a synchronized “hand + scene” dataset. Review and export the data in a standard format, ready for imitation learning.

3. Train & Validate

Plug the dataset into your behavior cloning or reinforcement learning pipeline. Quickly train and evaluate your robot’s grasping skills, with measurable improvements over baseline data.
